Neat Run

Neat Run is a jumping game, the objective of this game is to not hit any of the enemies coming towards you, the longer you stay alive the more intense the game gets, this includes speed of the enemies and the rate they come at you. I designed this game for a friend of mine who wanted something to mess about on, he gave me the idea of the jumper games, so i went and researched then finding a guide on how to create one and following along.
