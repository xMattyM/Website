Hero

Hero was the first game I created using python, it's a platformer game to reach the exit to get onto the next level, you need to be careful not to hit any of the enemies or lava along your way. When I started designing hero the guide I followed led me down one amazing path, looking at how far I've come along the adventure certainly inspired me to take it to the next level, after doing some research I bumped into Unity which is where my taste for game design went further.


Neat Run

Neat Run is a jumping game, the objective of this game is to not hit any of the enemies coming towards you, the longer you stay alive the more intense the game gets, this includes speed of the enemies and the rate they come at you. I designed this game for a friend of mine who wanted something to mess about on, he gave me the idea of the jumper game, so i went and researched then finding a guide on how to create one and following along.
